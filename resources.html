Resources

Competitive programming is a very recognized sport worldwide and so, a simple google search will result into tons of resources. However, for someone with no prior experience or knowledge in competitive programming it might be a bit difficult to get directed to the appropriate resource the individual is looking for. So, I tried to compile some of the resources into categories. Even for an experienced competitive programmer, these resources might come in handy while taking preparations for programming contests.
